Jaipreet Singh

Software Developer

Hey there, my name is Jaipreet Singh and I’m a recent engineering graduate looking to establish myself in the tech industry as a software developer. I specialize in web technologies and I'm passionate about creating memorable visual experiences!

I was introduced to software development when I took my first introductory programming course in University where we learned C++, and I instantly fell in love with the concept of coding. Throughout the rest of my curriculum I’d take on programming roles in group projects and seek out ways to apply my programming skills at internships.

While my programming courses at school didn’t go beyond elementary concepts, I would teach myself different languages and more advanced concepts in my free time. I eventually found myself getting into web development, and given my extensive background in graphic design and rigorous engineering curriculum, it felt like the perfect path to pursue moving forward! Some of my development projects can be seen below on my Github.

Github

I’ve always been a creative person at my core. As a child I would spend hours drawing in my sketchbooks, taking inspiration from my favourite cartoons and anime. Around 2015, I became interested in content creation on YouTube and joined a few friends to create videos and share them on our channels which is when I picked up graphic design and video editing.

I started exploring various areas of design, learning fundamental layout and typography concepts, colour theory, etc. out of pure passion for the field. I shared my work on social media and after building a following I began taking commissions for my services. I was able to work with established brands, build new brand identities, and interface with clients. My graphic design work can be viewed on my Behance profile.
