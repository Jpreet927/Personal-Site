Harmoni - AI Based Health Assistant

Senior capstone project

Harmoni is an app developed in a team of 4 to assist users in tracking their health and fitness progress over time. There is a rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases such as diabetes and stroke, and our team's commitment is to design a solution to provide an easy, simple product for individuals to take the next step in their journey towards improved health.

Team Members: Anuj Patel, Ireni Ruthirakuhan, Jaipreet Singh, Raj Parikh

Entropy Gaming

Brand Identity

Entropy Gaming is a professional gaming organization based in Germany, with focus in esports such as CounterStrike, League of Legends, and PUBG. Brand Identity Proposal

The Chicago Huntsmen are a North America based gaming team participating in the Call of Duty League.
